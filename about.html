Our Story

Real estate that feels personal again

FavHomes started in 2007 with a simple belief: buying or selling a home shouldn't feel like a transaction. It's one of the biggest moments in your life, and it deserves a team that treats it that way.

Eighteen years and 1,200 homes later, we still work the same way. We know these neighborhoods block by block, we answer our own phones, and we tell you the truth even when it costs us the sale.
